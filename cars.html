Effects

When heat reaches earth, it can come in, but it can’t get out because of these greenhouse gases. This can and does create numerous effects on our climate and future.

What are greenhouse gasses?

Greenhouse gasses are gasses that trap heat in Earth’s atmosphere. They let heat into our atmosphere but don’t let heat escape. Some of these gasses include methane, nitrous oxide and carbon dioxide. Greenhouse gasses are normally a good thing in the right quantities, they help our earth not get too cold, but with too many, they overheat our planet.
